About

Agony is an FPS game survival horror game where players begin their adventure as a tormented soul without any memories. Your ability to controlling people and possessing demons gives you the strength to survive. You should have in mind that Agony is quite an old school game, with the semi-open world, many paths, and endings. There are bad video games, and there is the world of Agony. The game is available on Steam, and the story has various endings, depending on the choices and progress the player makes.
